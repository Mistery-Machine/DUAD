Most Used Social Networks

What is a social network? A social network is a social structure made up
of a set of social actors (such as individuals or organizations) and a set
of the dyadic ties between these actors. The social network perspective
provides a set of methods for analyzing the structure of whole social
entities as well as a variety of theories explaining the patterns observed
in these structures.
